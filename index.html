What kind of companies do you work with? +

We work with early-stage companies (pre-seed to Series A) that need structured thinking around GTM, product, and growth. If you're a funded founder building something ambitious, we're likely a good fit. We're looking for founders who have already built an MVP and raised some capital so we can actually build traction together.

Are you an agency or a consultancy? +

Honestly, neither. Agencies try to sell you retainer hours and juniors to do the work. Consultants give you slides and leave. We embed with you as fractional partners. We're in your Slack, we write the emails, we audit the code, and we help you hire. We operate like temporary co-founders.

How long are typical engagements? +

We start with a 12-week GTM Sprint. It's a tight, focused window to build the GTM engine and get early traction. If we work well together, we transition to an ongoing Growth Partnership on a monthly rolling basis. We require a minimum 3-month commitment because anything less doesn't give us enough time to move the needle.

What does the process look like? +

You fill out the application below. We review it and get back to you within 48 hours. If we're a fit, we'll schedule a call to talk about your challenges and scope the project. No endless sales cycles — just an honest conversation about whether we can help you win.

What makes Think Layer different? +

We've actually built and scaled startups ourselves. We've stayed up until 3 AM debugging launch issues, cried over lost accounts, and celebrated early revenue. We don't hand you theoretical frameworks — we hand you operational engines that work.

What is your minimum monthly retainer? +

Engagements start at $5,000/month for a 12-week GTM Sprint. Ongoing retainers are $8,000–$10,000/month. We ask for a 3-month minimum because traction takes time to build and systemize.

Do you work with pre-revenue companies? +

Yes, absolutely — if you've raised funding and have a product people can actually use. If you're just at the idea stage and haven't built anything yet, we're not the right fit. You need to be post-MVP.
