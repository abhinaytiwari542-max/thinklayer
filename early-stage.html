The Context

SecondoBrain is an AI-powered platform designed to function as a company's "second brain" — managing tasks, decisions, and workflows intelligently. The vision is to make organizational thinking and execution seamless through AI integration.

My Role

I'm building SecondoBrain end-to-end. This is a full-ownership role spanning product vision, MVP development, core workflow design, and user acquisition.

What I'm Doing

  • Developing the MVP and defining core product workflows from scratch
  • Integrating AI into task management and decision-support systems
  • Building the product architecture and user experience end-to-end
  • Onboarding and testing with 100+ early users to validate and iterate

Current Status

SecondoBrain is currently in its MVP phase with 100+ early users actively testing the platform. The focus is on validating core AI-driven workflows and iterating on user feedback to refine the product before scaling.

Key Insight

The best AI products don't replace human thinking — they augment it. Building an AI "brain" means understanding how teams actually make decisions, then designing systems that make those decisions faster and clearer.
